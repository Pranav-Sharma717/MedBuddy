Yogic Exercises

Explore different yogic exercises below:

Surya Namaskar

A full-body exercise that improves flexibility and strength.

Tadasana

A standing pose that improves posture and helps with balance.

Vrikshasana

A tree pose that strengthens the legs and improves concentration.

Adho Mukha Svanasana

A downward dog pose that stretches the entire body.

Bhujangasana

A cobra pose that opens up the chest and strengthens the spine.

Setu Bandhasana

A bridge pose that helps strengthen the back and glutes.
